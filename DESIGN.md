# PreLoved Market — Design System

| Role           | Color         | Hex     |

| Background     | Light Gray    | #F9FAFB |

---

## Typography

* Font Family: Inter / Roboto (clean sans-serif)
* Headings: Bold, large size
* Subheadings: Medium weight
* Body Text: Regular weight
* Small Text: Light gray for secondary info

---

## Component Style

* Buttons:

  * Rounded corners (12–16px radius)
  * Filled primary buttons (Indigo background, white text)
  * Secondary buttons (outlined or light background)

* Cards:

  * White background
  * Rounded corners (12–16px)
  * Soft shadow for elevation
  * Used for product listings and reviews

* Input Fields:

  * Rounded edges
  * Light border
  * Error message shown below input

---

## Design Rationale

We chose a clean and modern design to make the app easy to use for students with different levels of digital experience. The indigo primary color gives a sense of trust and reliability, which is important for a marketplace where users interact and exchange items. Soft background colors and white cards help keep the interface uncluttered and readable, especially on low-end devices. Rounded components and simple layouts create a friendly and approachable feel. The design also focuses on clarity, ensuring that important information like price (in RWF), size, and condition is easy to see. Overall, the goal was to create a lightweight, user-friendly, and trustworthy student marketplace experience.
